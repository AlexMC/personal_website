# Podroll

## Podcasts I Listen To

My regular podcast rotation.

### Technology

- [AppStories](https://appstories.net/) - Dive into the world of apps form a user standpoint
- [MacStories Unwind](https://www.macstories.net/unwind/) - Weekly unwind media recommendations
- [No priors](https://linktr.ee/nopriors) - Deep dives in to AI by interviewing relevant AI people
- [Bankless](https://www.bankless.com/podcast) - The latest info on Blockchain world (Heavely ethereum focused)
- [Diggnation](https://www.diggnation.show/) - A fun way to learn the latest tech news
- [The talk show with John Grubber](https://daringfireball.net/thetalkshow/) - Weekly interviews with relevant people from the Apple indie community.
- [Tech Brew ride home](https://www.ridehome.info/show/techmeme-ride-home/) - Daily 15 minutes episodes with the latest tech news


### Gaming

- [Next Portable Console](https://www.macstories.net/npc/) - Weekly podcast focused on handheld gaming
- [Into the Aether](https://intothecast.online/) - By gamers for gamers, "a low key video game podcast"
