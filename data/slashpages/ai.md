# AI

## How I Use AI

Although I'm not oblivious of the problems arising from the intelectual property misappropriation by the AI model training corps, I've seen enough to believe there is no escape from a future where AI is omnipresent on everyone's life. 
		  
To that effect I'm committed to learn, understand, use and effectively leverage AI tooling to achieve my personal goals.

At this point in time this mostly means I'm reading about and experimenting with all kinds of AI tools on a daily basis.
