Podcasts I Listen To

My regular podcast rotation.

Technology

  • AppStories - Dive into the world of apps form a user standpoint
  • MacStories Unwind - Weekly unwind media recommendations
  • No priors - Deep dives in to AI by interviewing relevant AI people
  • Bankless - The latest info on Blockchain world (Heavely ethereum focused)
  • Diggnation - A fun way to learn the latest tech news
  • The talk show with John Grubber - Weekly interviews with relevant people from the Apple indie community.
  • Tech Brew ride home - Daily 15 minutes episodes with the latest tech news

Gaming

  • Next Portable Console - Weekly podcast focused on handheld gaming
  • Into the Aether - By gamers for gamers, "a low key video game podcast"

Local (Portuguese)

  • Assim vamos ter que falar de outra maneira - A comedy podcast by some of the most brilliant Portuguese comedians.
  • Expresso da Manhã - Light political daily podcast.
  • O Homem que comia tudo - Foodie podcast.
  • Programa cujo nome estamos legalmente impedidos de dizer - Political podcast discussing the weekly topics but in the lightest way possible.

Other

  • Criminal - A podcast about crime super well produced
  • Planet Money - The world trough the lens of economy
  • Science Vs - Using proper science to distinguish facts from fakes.
  • Freakanomics Radio - Economics driven storytelling
  • Radiolab - Radiolab explores big ideas through stories that blend science, philosophy, and sound.
  • Search Engine - Tries to answer the craziest questions that keeps ou uup at night.
  • The Skeptics guide to the universe - Promotes critical thinking and science by examining extraordinary claims, debunking pseudoscience and sharing cool science news.
  • This American Life - Compelling, real-life stories that reveal the extraordinary in the ordinary.

Recommendations Welcome
